WHAT IS MEAN BY AUTOCAD

  • AutoCAD is a computer-aided design (CAD) software applicat developed by Autodesk. It's used by architects, engineers, drafters, and other professionals to create precise 2D and 3D drawings.
  • Here's what AutoCAD can do:2D Drafting: Create detailed drawings with tools to draw lines, arcs, circles, and other geometric shapes.
  • 3D Modeling: Design in three dimensions, allowing users to create 3D models of buildings, products, or any other object.
  • Rendering: Generate realistic renderings of designs, which helps in visualizing how a finished product or building will look.
  • Annotation: Add text, dimensions, and annotations to drawings to provide detailed information.
  • Collaboration: AutoCAD allows for easy sharing of designs, making it easier for teams to work together on projects.
  • It's one of the most widely used CAD programs in the world due to its versatility and range of tools for creating technical drawings and designs.
